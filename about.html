Hello, hello!

I'm a product designer in the Bay, with lots to say.

I recently graduated from UC Berkeley with degrees in Data Science & Critical Design Studies. Last summer I was a Product Design intern at Dropbox NYC; the summer before that, at IBM Design. Previously, I served as president of Berkeley Inno­vation and design director at Skydeck.

I am also interested in philosophy & critical theory, new media, and design ethics. Altogether, I am interested in design theory, and its broad role in forming contemporary socio-technical discourse — and the theories of subjectivity that undergird design practice. My greatest inspirations are Joanna Newsom and Charli XCX.
