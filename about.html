Lian Song

Me, circa 2017

I am fascinated by the ever blurring lines between technology and human cognition — particularly how recent technological advancements have fundamentally altered the way in which communities and identities interact with the world around them. I am also interested in the ethics of Silicon Valley and the tech industry; recently, I attended O4U, a wonderful conference where I was able to speak to other queer students and professionals about life in tech.

Beyond that, I am deeply interested in philosophy and critical theory; you can catch me at a used book store, probably holding something by Foucault.

In my free time, I'm off shooting photographs, sharpening my web design skills, and seeking new spaces to learn and create in. I adore the beautiful folk singer Sufjan Stevens, birds and octopus, and anything written by Murakami.

If you'd like to connect, ping me at hello@liansong.co!
