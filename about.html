Hello.

I'm a Product Designer in the Bay, out here looking to create killer design experiences.

I study Cognitive & Data Science at UC Berkeley. Last summer I was a Product Design intern at IBM Design, and currently I serve as president of Berkeley Inno­vation, the design consultancy at UC Berkeley. I am also design director at Skydeck, and a designer at Naming Matters.

More importantly, I'm interested in philosophy, queer(ing) bodies, new media, and design ethics. Altogether, I am in design theory, and its broad role in social movement. My greatest inspirations are Joanna Newsom and the tomato section at Berkeley Bowl.
