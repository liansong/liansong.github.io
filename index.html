About

Hi! My name is Lian. It's a pleasure to meet you.

Currently, I am a student at UC Berkeley studying Computer & Cognitive Science, with a focus on Human Centered Design. I strive to craft emotive design solutions — to that end, I work as a product designer and serve as Director of Sponsorships for Berkeley Innovation, a student-run design consultancy at Berkeley.

Beyond design, I am deeply interested in intelligence, especially its philosophical and computational underpinnings. In my free time, I shoot photographs and seek out new cafés to learn and create in.

I am currently looking for internship opportunites for Summer '17.
