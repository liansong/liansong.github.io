About Lian

Hiya!


I'm a designer in my second year at UC Berkeley, studying Computer & Cognitive Science with a particular interest in Design Innovation. I am fascinated by the ever blurring lines between technology and human cognition, and how technology can be used to augment and embody the intractable mind.

I strive to create emotive and accessible design solutions — to that end, I work as a product designer and serve as Director of Marketing for Berkeley Innovation, a student-run design consultancy here at Berkeley. In addition, I work as a web designer for clients at Berkeley as a part of Innovative Design.

When I'm not 20 layers deep in my latest Sketch project, I'm out shooting photographs or seeking new cafés to learn and create in. I also adore Sufjan Stevens, good coffee, birds and octopus, and anything written by Murakami.

I am excited to be joining Rainforest QA this summer as a Product Design intern! Feel free to check out my resume and LinkedIn, and don't hesitate to shoot me a message at hello@liansong.co!
