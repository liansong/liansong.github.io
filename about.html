Hiya! I'm a second year at UC Berkeley, studying Cognitive Science & Design Innovation. Last summer, I worked as a Product Design Intern at Rainforest QA; at school, I serve as the Director of Marketing and a project manager for Berkeley Innovation, and previously I was a visual & web designer at Innovative Design.

I am fascinated by the blurring lines between technology and human cognition — in particular, how recent technological advancements have shaped the way in which communities and identities percieve themselves. I am also interested in expression and identity in the tech industry; recently, I attended O4U Tech, a wonderful conference where I was able to speak to students and professionals about life and authenticity.

In my free time, I'm off shooting photographs, studying philosophy, sharpening my web design skills, and seeking new spaces to learn and create in. I adore the beautiful folk singer Sufjan Stevens, birds and octopus, and anything written by Murakami.
