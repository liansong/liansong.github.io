Flyers & Logos

Miscellaneous designs
2015 - 2017

Women in Computer Science

A logo I created for the Women in Computer Science club at UIUC. The inspiration for this design stemmed from a desire to visually portray women as belonging within the CS community. I approached that quite literally with this logo — the face is between the ‘C’ and the ‘S’ formed by the hair.

Monta Vista Senior Ball

An informational flyer that doubled as a ticket, handed out to attendees of my high school’s Senior Ball. The skyline at the bottom is the San Francisco skyline, advertising the location of the event — on a cruise ship in the SF bay. The event was wonderful, if you were wondering (I planned it!).

Berkeley Innovation Career Fair

A promotional flyer created for the Berkeley Innovation Design Career Fair, which I planned and organized. Also, quick plug — if you or your company is interested in working with Berkeley Innovation, shoot me an email! We do all kinds of stuff, from tech talks and career fairs to design conferences!

CED Undergrad Student Council

A set of logos that I created for the CED Undergraduate Student Council at UC Berkeley — a colored option and a monochrome option. The colors are meant to represent the fields of study that come together under CED, and the monochrome represents common patterns seen in architectural tools.
